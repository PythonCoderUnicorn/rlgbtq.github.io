RainbowR

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote queer peoples in the R community, provide a supportive and safe environment for people of any gender identity or orientation, and spread awareness of queer issues through data-driven activism.


Involvement

If you want to get involved, please click below for an invitation to our Slack. We welcome all members, queer or not, who are interested in promoting queer people and issues in the R community and beyond!

Partnerships

We partner with fellow R diversity and inclusive orientated groups such as Forwards R, Minorities in R and any others.
